`assets/bacteria_sheet.png` was baked (8 walk phases × 8 view angles,
posed and rendered in Blender) from the rigged model:

- **"Bacteria [Back rooms] rigged (Blender 3.01)"** — Sketchfab:
  <https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/bacteria-back-rooms-rigged-blender-301>
  Used under the license stated on its Sketchfab page; all credit to its
  author. The creature design originates from **Kane Pixels'** Backrooms
  series — this project is unaffiliated fan work.

The model file itself is not distributed in this repository — only the
baked silhouette sprites.
